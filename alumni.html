Professional Networking

Cal Lacrosse is not just a collegiate lacrosse team, its a program; a network of players, parents, alumni and coaches that share a passion for excellence on and off the field. The main function of our Professional Network is to support current players in securing summer internships and transitioning from Cal into the workforce. If your firm has internship or job openings, please fill out the Alumni Connect form, or email Sam Tobis with a brief description. Additionally, if you are an alumnus looking to transition into a new role or a new industry, connect to Cal Lacrosse on LinkedIn, Facebook or email Sam for additional networking opportunities. Thank you for keeping our community strong and vibrant.
