History of Cal Men's Lacrosse

The University of California Mens Lacrosse Club Team prides itself on a winning tradition. In 1980, Cal was a founding member of the Western Collegiate Lacrosse Association (WCLL). The WCLL consists of Cal, Cal Poly, Sonoma State, Chico State, Santa Clara, Davis, Stanford. Each year, Cal competes for the WCLL title and a bid to the national tournament. Today, the WCLL is one of the strongest conferences in the MCLA with 5 teams ranked in the top 25. In addition to WCLL opponents, Cal frequently matches up with top teams from around the country including teams from Oregon, Arizona, Utah, Minnesota, Texas, and New York.



WCLL Champions: 1981, 1984, 1994, 1995, 1998, 2000, 2004.

National Champions: 1998
