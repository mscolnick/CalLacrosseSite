50th Anniversary Weekend
April 25th, 26th and 27th, 2014

Friday: Championship Cocktails

WHEN: Friday April 25, 7 - 9 PM
WHERE: The Paragon Bar at the Claremont Hotel, Berkeley
Join alumni to kick off the weekend with a bang.


Saturday: Lacrosse Game Day


WHEN: Saturday April 26, 3PM Tailgate 5PM game time, with trophy ceremony and reception to follow
WHERE: California Memorial Stadium, Field Club level

Don't miss the 50th Cal vs. Stanford Lacrosse game! Join us for a festive tailgate in the Memorial Stadium Field Club, senior day acknowledgements and Cal vs. Stanford Lacrosse game on Kabam field, and the resurrection of the Sadler Miller Broken Stick Trophy. Special alumni ceremony and photo opportunity at halftime.

$20 in advance, $30 at the door.
Tailgate Tickets
(parking passes available through April 22)

Sunday: Doug Sciutto Memorial Golf Tournament

WHEN: Sunday April 27, 11AM registration, 1PM tee off, 5PM cocktails and dinner
WHERE: Tilden Park Golf Course, Berkeley

Register for Golf

The Cal Lacrosse 50th Weekend is hosted by the Department of Recreational Sports, the Cal Men's Lacrosse Parent Boosters, and the Cal Men's Lacrosse Alumni Board. All proceeds from the weekend support the Cal Men's Lacrosse program.
